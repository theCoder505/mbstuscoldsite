Scholarship in MIT & USA

MIT Scholarships types Research Assistantships (RA) 
  1. ⦁ Doctoral- $37,128 per year 
  2. ⦁ Master’s- $33,924 per year Teaching Assistantships (TA) 
  3. ⦁ Contact- $38,016 per year 
  4. ⦁ Support- $34,272 per year 

Frankly speaking, MIT tuition fees are very high. Both domestic and international students need to bear this high amount. Approximately $50,000/year is required for both undergraduate and graduate degrees. However, It's one of the most prestigious and most significant scholarships all over the world. So, all we want this Scholarship.

For applying for the Scholarship, you need to follow these steps.
  1. ⦁ International students need to sit for a TOFEL examination where the domestic don’t need to.
  2. ⦁ International students need to make a new MIT Application account.
  3. ⦁ They need to submit their required documents with the application also.
  4. ⦁ As a student applies for MIT Scholarship s/he needs to complete the financial aid documents too.

Documents needed for International Students 

    Standardized Scores (one of two options) 
  1. ⦁ Option 1- SAT or ACT and two SAT Subject Tests (One in math and one in sciences).
  2. ⦁ Option 2- TOEFL (IELTS not accepted) and two SAT Subject Tests (One in math and one in sciences).
  3. ⦁ For more information about the standardized score requirements, visit the MIT website. 
  4. ⦁ Application Fee of $75 or Application Fee Waiver
  5. ⦁ Biographical Form
  6. ⦁ Essays, Activities, and Academics 
  7. ⦁ Evaluation of two teachers
  8. ⦁ Secondary School Report including high school transcripts
  9. ⦁ February Updates and Notes From (including midyear grades)
